Govmeeting - Access to your town or city council meetings

Using this site, you will be able to:

  • Browse transcripts of your town or city meetings. Browse by subject, speaker, etc. Browse meetings
  • Be notified when a specific topic is discussed at future meetings: Notifications

How it works:

  • Anyone can register their town or city to have their meeting information available here: Register my town or city

Sometimes your town or city may provide transcripts of meeetings. Sometimes only a video recording is available. At other times, neither may be available. We try to cover all three situations.

  • When only recordings of meeting are available, we will auto transcribe the recording. Volunteers will then proof-read the transcription.: Proof-read transcripts
  • Volunteers will tag the transcript with appropriate subject names: Tag transcripts
  • If neither online recordings or transcripts, a volunteer can use our mobile app to record a meeting and upload it: Record meeting
